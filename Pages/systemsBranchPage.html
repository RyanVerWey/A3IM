Systems and Architecture

Mission Statement

Provide timely and accurate foundational, anticipatory, and actionable intelligence analysis on Cyberspace Capabilities, Partnerships, Threats, And Vulnerabilities in the AOR to USCENTCOM, component forces, and U.S. decision makers to support the planning and execution of operations, policy development, and achievement of USCENTCOM objectives.

Priorities and Lines of Effort

Manages and enhances the J2 digital presence through IT, Web Development, and exercise support. The branch integrates intelligence platforms, applications, and communication capabilities to create effective solutions for intelligence information sharing in the CENTCOM AOR.

LOE 1 - Culture- Garner leadership buy-in by improving process throughout the J2

LOE 4 - Infrastructure and Tools- Identify hardware capacity/capability shortfalls and improve health of IT infrastructure

LOE 5 - Experimentation and Integration- Conduct process mapping interviews to improve the efficiency across all divisions within the J2, through innovation and digital improvements. Create systems architecture for coalition sharing during exercises and major CENTCOM plans
