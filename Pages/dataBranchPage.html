Data Management and Engineering

Mission Statement

Establish and maintain the Directorate Data Strategy, Data Management Processes, Standards, and Governance. Responsible for integrating CCJ2 data holdings and ensuring accessibility to the CCJ2 JWICS cloud architecture. Executes CCJ2 Development Security Operations; conducting secure development of advanced tools and algorithms to modernize CCJ2 analytic capabilities and analytic enabling processes.

Priorities and Lines of Effort

Utilize System Engineers, Data Scientists, and Cyber Security Experts to plan, code, build, test, deploy, operate, and monitor secure software products that have received Authority to Operate on JWICS, SIPRNet, or NIPRNet to CCJ2 or Command customers.

LOE 1 - Infrastructure Development - Operationalizing J28 data management platform - Lakehouse - on all three domains

LOE 2 - Management and Governance - Draft data strategy and governance along with required implementation plans.

LOE 3 - Mission Applications - Develop applications using Lakehouse DevSecOps pipeline based on validated requirements in our Agile Development Information System

LOE 4 - Data Security - Continuous re-evaluation of security requirements of Lakehouse and its applications

LOE 5 - Culture and Partnership Enablement - Keep pace and leverage innovation via established forums and trusted partnerships across the DoD and IC
