Open Source Intelligence

Mission Statement

Collect, process, and evaluate Publicly and Commercially Available Information to disseminate open source intelligence reporting that delivers insights, indicators, or warning for use in all-source analysis. This tailored reporting is focused on answering Priority Intelligence Requirements to support planning and executing operations, policy development, and the achievement of USCENTCOM objectives during routine and crisis operations.

Priorities and Lines of Effort

Collect and process PAI using DIA approved tools and managed attribution platforms during research to mitigate operational risk. Explore, evaluate, and advocate for new technologies to strengthen Combatant Command collection and exploitation capabilities. Produce OSINT reports (OSIRs) on Priority Intelligence Requirements that are fully available for use in finished intelligence products.

LOE 1 - Detering Iran by Providing Indications and Warning (I&W) Tippers and Open Source Intelligence Reports (OSIRs), identifying new data sources, and developing advanced collection capabilities that provide new insights and satisfy intelligence gaps

LOE 2 - Countering Violent Extremist Organizations by Providing Indications and Warning (I&W) Tippers and Open Source Intelligence Reports (OSIRs), identifying new data sources, and developing advanced collection capabilities that provide new insights and satisfy intelligence gaps

LOE 3 - Competing Strategy by Providing Indications and Warning (I&W) Tippers and Open Source Intelligence Reports (OSIRs), identifying new data sources, and developing advanced collection capabilities that provide new insights and satisfy intelligence gaps
