Mission Statement

Provide relevant, instructive, and contextual intelligence information on adversary and neutral force disposition, intentions, and supporting infrastructures derived from all sources at any classification level, shared by more than one command, that assists all echelons to achieve situational awareness and decision advantage within the CENTCOM AOR and AOI.

Priorities and Lines of Effort

Manages complex Near Real-Time (NRT) alert criteria related to heightened focuses and time-windows for active threat streams throughout the CENTCOM AOR.

LOE 1 - I&W of Iranian emerging all-domain threats and activities impacting U.S. interests

LOE 2 - Identification of no-notice/one-way UAS launch

LOE 3 - Provide context to enable decision-making space through activity-based intelligence methodology
